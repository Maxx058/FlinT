    • Sleepiest

    Sleep app that helps millions to fall asleep every night

    The Challenge

    Redesigning a mobile app with a multi-million user base is always a big responsibility. Users sometimes react negatively to changes in interactions that have become familiar, which can bring down ratings. Our team worked with Sleepiest to audition dozens of design concepts until we found the perfect solution for improving the design while keeping users comfortable with familiar interactions.

    Unique Experience

    Our first task was to give a makeover to Sleepiest’s logotype and corporate identity. We spruced up and cleaned up the lettering. The illustration style went through several phases and multiple iterations.Our team really got into the application’s mood. We were motivated to focus on every little detail including icon animation, to really enhance the user interaction.
